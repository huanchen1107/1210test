## Project Conventions

### OpenSpec Change Naming
All change IDs MUST follow this numbering format:
- Format: `{number}-{verb}-{description}`
- Number: Two-digit auto-incrementing (01, 02, 03, etc.)
- Verb: Use `add`, `update`, `remove`, or `refactor`
- Description: Kebab-case, short, descriptive

Examples:
- `01-add-user-authentication`
- `02-update-payment-flow`
- `03-remove-legacy-api`

**Next available number: 02**
